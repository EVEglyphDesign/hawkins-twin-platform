What happens after you send

  1. Each artifact is filed into the private compliance vault at EVEglyphDesign/hawkins-twin-compliance-vault. The repository is the archive of record.
  2. You are copied on the archive confirmation so you have a receipt in your inbox.
  3. When all sixteen are in, Dany closes the intake and moves to the next phase (twin build, public disclosure, and audit posture).

Why the repository — and not a portal

This intake is deliberately low-tech. A PDF and a URL sent by email, filed to a repository you can inspect, is defensible in a way that a fancy form is not. The point is that the archive is honest, complete, and yours to audit. Nothing lives inside a chat window or a vendor's dashboard where it can be quietly changed.
