Hawkins Twin Platform

Single surface in front. Durable repositories underneath. A dealer-grade digital twin aligned to Peterbilt, PACCAR, warranty, maintenance, financing, and recycling — anchored where the work actually happens.

What it is

The public experience is intentionally simple: one link, one explanation, one place to understand what the platform is for. Underneath that surface, the asset is structured as twin repositories that represent equipment, vehicles, warranty actions, maintenance flows, financing events, and recycling obligations in a durable form.

The framing is dealer-anchored on purpose. The dealer sits at the intersection of the vehicle, the warranty, the parts economy, and the recycling obligation — every one of those is a durable relationship no OEM can replicate directly. That is the honest place to represent them.

Platform

Equipment & vehicles

Peterbilt units, PACCAR powertrains, dealer-anchored records that survive resale and re-registration.

Warranty actions

Claim history, PACCAR Solutions integration, warranty exposure held at the dealer surface.

Maintenance flows

SmartLINQ-informed service intervals, parts fitment, technician assignment — twin-tracked against the physical unit.

Financing events

Origination, refinance, trade-in, and end-of-lease represented as events on the same vehicle twin.

Recycling & end-of-life

Second-life battery routing, parts recovery, end-of-life documentation. A revenue and stewardship layer, not an afterthought.

Charging & infrastructure

Depot charging, corridor MCS sites, second-life stationary storage — natural extensions of the dealer footprint.

Applications

  • Dealer-anchored twin for Peterbilt / PACCAR equipment lifecycle.
  • Warranty and service history that stays with the unit, not the paperwork.
  • Recycling and second-life battery workflows integrated with the service bay.
  • Handoff surface for logistics resilience research, corridor buildout notes, and freight electrification reference material.
