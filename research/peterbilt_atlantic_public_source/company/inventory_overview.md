# Peterbilt Atlantic — Inventory & Product Lines

**Sources:** [peterbiltatlantic.com](https://www.peterbiltatlantic.com), blog posts, inventory listings, ZoomInfo, Peterbilt Motors product descriptions

## Overview

Peterbilt Atlantic is the **exclusive authorized Peterbilt dealer** for Atlantic Canada (New Brunswick, Nova Scotia, Prince Edward Island, Newfoundland & Labrador) and Eastern Quebec. The company sells and services:
- New Peterbilt trucks (all classes and models available through PACCAR/Peterbilt)
- Pre-owned / used trucks
- Commercial trailers (Rockico Trailer Sales division)
- Parts and accessories (Peterbilt OE and TRP aftermarket)
- Fleet management technology (FleetRight)
- Financing (PACCAR Financial)
- Leasing (PacLease franchise — described as "the region's largest PacLease franchise" per [ZoomInfo](https://www.zoominfo.com/c/peterbilt-atlantic/345268680))

## New Truck Models

### Class 8 — Highway / Sleeper

#### Peterbilt Model 579
- The flagship aerodynamic sleeper cab; Peterbilt's most fuel-efficient Class 8 long-haul model.
- Available with **PACCAR MX-13** or **Cummins X15** engine options.
- Available sleeper configurations include the **Ultraloft 80** (premium large-format sleeper).
- A live **2026 Peterbilt 579** listing was confirmed at Hanwell: Stock #TD782692, Cummins X15, Ultraloft 80 sleeper, 450 HP, all-aluminum wheels, Low Air Leaf suspension, 12-speed transmission. ([Inventory listing](https://www.peterbiltatlantic.com/New-Inventory-2026-Peterbilt-Heavy-Duty-Truck-579-Hanwell-New-Brunswick-17373482))
- A **2025 Peterbilt 579** was also listed with PACCAR MX-13, Ultraloft 80, 455 HP.
- A **579 Ultraloft demo** was offered for test drive (~September 2025, per blog listing).
- The blog post "New Peterbilt Model 579 Unveiled" (Feb. 2021) announced the model's introduction to the Peterbilt Atlantic lineup.

#### Peterbilt Model 589
- The **successor to the legendary Model 389**, introduced in Eastern Canada by Peterbilt Atlantic in **January 2024**.
- First Eastern Canada 589 sold to **Mutch Trucking Ltd.** ([Blog, Jan. 2024](https://www.peterbiltatlantic.com/blog/new-model-589--67103))
- Features the classic long-nose "conventional" styling of the 389, expanded from the 1.9m to the **2.1m platform**.
- Described as offering "best-in-class uptime, competitive total cost of ownership advantage, and ease of serviceability."
- Available for spec and order at all Peterbilt Atlantic locations.

#### Peterbilt Model 389 / 389X (Legacy / Collector)
- The Model 389 was one of the most iconic trucks in the Peterbilt lineup for decades; production ended and the last Eastern Canada example was sold to **WCF Transport** (James Kinsman, Berwick, NS) by Peterbilt Atlantic in **February 2025** ([Blog, Feb. 2025](https://www.peterbiltatlantic.com/blog/sunset-on-the-model-389--92309)).
- The **Limited Edition 389X** (only 1,389 units worldwide) was available for order through Peterbilt Atlantic in September 2022.
- Multiple "New Truck" blog posts from 2022 document Model 389 deliveries to: Leader by Litre, Trent Hill, Rose's Mobile 1, Fundy Express Transport, Ocean Crisp Transport Ltd, Parker Trucking, Robbins Trucking, Belgo Transport, JE March, and Kingharv Inc.

### Electric Vehicles (EV Lineup)

Peterbilt Atlantic became the authorized EV dealer for Eastern Canada starting in 2021. All three electric models below are available for order across NB, NS, PEI, NL, and QC. Primary EV contact: Craig Allen, callen@peterbiltatlantic.com / 506-451-2001.

#### Peterbilt Model 579EV
- **Class:** Class 8, heavy-duty
- **Range:** Up to 250 km
- **Charging:** DC fast-charge in 3–4 hours
- **Use cases:** Regional haul, drayage, pickup and delivery, last-mile
- **Powertrain:** Fully integrated all-electric with Lithium Iron Phosphate (LFP) battery packs; designed for optimal weight distribution.
- **Source:** [Blog, Apr. 2021](https://www.peterbiltatlantic.com/blog/peterbilt-electric-trucks--34720)

#### Peterbilt Model 520EV
- **Class:** Heavy duty refuse/vocational
- **Range:** 160 km; 1,100 bin cycles (automated side loader) or 130 compactor cycles (rear loader refuse body)
- **Charging:** DC fast-charge in 3–4 hours
- **Use cases:** Refuse collection (predictable daily routes)
- **Source:** [Blog, Apr. 2021](https://www.peterbiltatlantic.com/blog/peterbilt-electric-trucks--34720)

#### Peterbilt Model 220EV
- **Class:** Class 6/7, medium duty
- **Range:** Up to 320 km on a full charge
- **Charging:** DC fast-charge in 1–2 hours; also has on-board charger
- **Use cases:** Local pickup and delivery, short regional haul
- **Powertrain:** Electric motor with two battery packs
- **Demo availability:** A 220EV demo truck was made available at Peterbilt Atlantic in May 2024 ([Blog, May 2024](https://www.peterbiltatlantic.com/blog/category/trucks--1228)).
- **Customer test:** The YouTube video "Peterbilt 220EV Test with Picaroons Brewing Company" shows the 220EV in use by a real Atlantic Canadian customer.
- **EV Cost Calculator:** Peterbilt Motors hosts an operating cost calculator for the 220EV, 520EV, and 579EV models. Blog post: [peterbiltatlantic.com/blog/peterbilt-ev-operating-calculator--46320](https://www.peterbiltatlantic.com/blog/peterbilt-ev-operating-calculator--46320)
- **Source:** [Blog, Apr. 2021](https://www.peterbiltatlantic.com/blog/peterbilt-electric-trucks--34720)

### Canadian Manufacturing Milestone (2025)

As of October 2025, **Class 8 Peterbilt trucks are now being manufactured at PACCAR's Sainte-Thérèse, QC facility**, making PACCAR/Peterbilt the only OEM building both heavy- and medium-duty trucks in Canada. Peterbilt Atlantic published two blog posts about this milestone: "Built in Canada — Driven by You" (Oct. 26, 2025) and "Class 8 Trucks Built in Canada by Peterbilt" (Sep. 29, 2025). ([Blog](https://www.peterbiltatlantic.com/blog/built-in-canada---driven-by-you--101690))
