## 1. Peterbilt Atlantic (Core Operating Entity)

**Legal/operating name:** Peterbilt Atlantic  
**Type:** Private company (Corporation, per BBB file for affiliated entity)  
**Headquarters:** 125 Greenview Drive (TransCanada Exit 281), Hanwell, New Brunswick, E3C  
**Previous HQ address:** 45 Gillis Road, Fredericton, NB, E3C (used until June 2017)  
**Toll-free:** 1-800-922-7383  
**Web:** [peterbiltatlantic.com](https://www.peterbiltatlantic.com)  
**French-language site:** [fr.peterbiltatlantic.com](https://fr.peterbiltatlantic.com)

**D&B listing principal:** Tim Hawkins, President ([D&B](https://www.dnb.com/business-directory/company-profiles.peterbilt_atlantic.1793edc8fb5e065a90469e753318cb9b.html))  
**ZoomInfo profile:** [ZoomInfo](https://www.zoominfo.com/c/peterbilt-atlantic/345268680) — lists $14.5M revenue; 87 employees (methodologically narrower than the company's own stated 160+)  
**Industry classification (D&B):** Automobile Dealers; Motor Vehicle and Parts Dealers; Trucks, tractors, and trailers: new and used  
**Industry (ZoomInfo):** Automobile Dealers

**Parent/franchisor:** Peterbilt Atlantic is an authorized dealer of **Peterbilt Motors Company**, which is a wholly-owned subsidiary of **PACCAR Inc.** (NASDAQ: PCAR — publicly traded US company, headquartered in Bellevue, WA). Peterbilt Atlantic is an independent regional dealer, not a PACCAR subsidiary; it holds an exclusive Peterbilt dealership franchise for Atlantic Canada and Eastern Quebec.

**Founding history:** Tim Hawkins and his father launched the first location in Fredericton, New Brunswick in **1994** ([About page](https://www.peterbiltatlantic.com/about-us-heavy-trucks-trailers-dealership--info)). The family brought "three generations and 45 years of experience" in the truck business at founding. Truck News (2014) confirms "Hawkins' father started dealing Petes in New Brunswick in 1994."

**Growth timeline:**
- **1994:** First Fredericton location (Hawkins family)
- **~2011:** Kentville, NS acquired ("three years ago" per 2014 Truck News piece)
- **~2013:** Quebec dealership acquired (Truck News 2014: "last year, they bought a Quebec dealership")
- **~2014:** PEI rights held; Moncton formal opening under Peterbilt Atlantic brand (6 locations, 120 employees)
- **2017:** New Hanwell facility opens; Saint-Pascal and Saint-Louis confirmed as nine total locations; nine-location era begins
- **2024:** New Dartmouth facility opens (65 Gloria McCluskey Avenue, November 4, 2024)

## 3. PACCAR Financial (Financing Partner)

**Relationship:** Primary financing partner for Peterbilt Atlantic customer transactions  
**Type:** PACCAR Inc. subsidiary (publicly traded on NASDAQ: PCAR)  
**Description:** Provides retail loans, leases, and insurance products for Peterbilt truck purchases at Peterbilt Atlantic ([Financing page](https://www.peterbiltatlantic.com/financing))  
**Note:** PACCAR Financial is a national/international entity; its relationship to Peterbilt Atlantic is as a preferred lender, not a corporate parent or owner.

## 4. PacLease (Leasing Franchise)

**Relationship:** Peterbilt Atlantic holds a PacLease franchise and is described as "the region's largest PacLease franchise" ([ZoomInfo](https://www.zoominfo.com/c/peterbilt-atlantic/345268680))  
**PacLease** is PACCAR's full-service truck leasing and rental program, offering customized leasing agreements for commercial trucking operators.

## 5. Rockico Trailer Sales (Division / Partner)

**Relationship:** Referenced on the Peterbilt Atlantic website as a trailer sales division ([Blog, trailer category](https://www.peterbiltatlantic.com/blog/category/parts--1231))  
**Description:** ZoomInfo describes Peterbilt Atlantic as "the region's source for custom tractor trailers, Caterpillar, Cummins and Rockico products." Rockico appears to be a trailer brand/division sold through the dealership. Blog content (2017–2018) references "Rockico Trailer Sales" selling construction equipment trailers, flatbeds, and the Talbert Rockico Custom Deck (a 65-ton heavy-haul float). A 2012 YouTube video confirms Peterbilt Atlantic and Rockico Trailers appeared jointly at the Atlantic Heavy Equipment Show.

## 6. PACCAR Inc. (Franchisor / Manufacturer)

**Relationship:** Peterbilt Atlantic holds an authorized exclusive dealership franchise from Peterbilt Motors Company, itself a wholly owned subsidiary of PACCAR Inc.  
**PACCAR Inc.:**
- Headquarters: Bellevue, WA, USA
- NASDAQ: PCAR
- Manufactures Peterbilt and Kenworth trucks; Peterbilt medium-duty trucks manufactured at Sainte-Thérèse, QC facility; Class 8 trucks now also produced at Sainte-Thérèse as of 2025.
- PACCAR Financial is the captive finance arm.
- PACCAR is described as "the only OEM building both heavy- and medium-duty trucks in Canada" ([Blog, Oct. 2025](https://www.peterbiltatlantic.com/blog/built-in-canada---driven-by-you--101690)).

## 7. Summary of Ownership / Control Structure

Based on all publicly available sources, the structure visible from public records is:

```
Timothy Hawkins
├── CEO — Peterbilt Atlantic (January 1993 – present)
│     ├── 9 locations: Hanwell NB, Moncton NB, Charlottetown PE, Dartmouth NS,
│     │   Kentville NS, Sydney NS, Deer Lake NL, Saint-Pascal QC, Saint-Louis-du-Ha!Ha! QC
│     ├── PacLease franchise (largest in region)
│     └── Rockico Trailer Sales division
└── CEO / Director — Extreme Torque Motorsports (January 2010 – present)
      ├── 3 locations: Moncton NB, Hanwell NB (133 Greenview Dr), Woodstock NB
      └── Brands: Can-Am, Sea-Doo, Ski-Doo, Lynx, Manitou
```

No holding company name, parent corporation, or private equity backing is visible in any public filing or data source reviewed. Both operating entities appear to be held directly by Timothy Hawkins.

**Note:** The Hawkins family ("three generations" per Tim Hawkins' own About page text) was involved in the truck business before 1994; the specific legal structure of the original family business that pre-dated Peterbilt Atlantic is not described in any accessible public source.
