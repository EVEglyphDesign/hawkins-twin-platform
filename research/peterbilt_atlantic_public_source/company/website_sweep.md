## 1. Homepage

**URL:** [https://www.peterbiltatlantic.com](https://www.peterbiltatlantic.com)

**Tagline:** "At Home on the Road"

**Summary:** The homepage introduces Peterbilt Atlantic as Eastern Canada's authoritative heavy-truck dealer with eight active locations in five provinces (New Brunswick, Nova Scotia, Prince Edward Island, Newfoundland & Labrador, and Quebec). The copy emphasizes proximity to highway exits, modern facilities, comfortable drivers' lounges, certified technicians, OE parts, and a "down east" hospitality brand promise. A rotating blog feed displays the five most recent posts.

**Meta description (site-wide):** "Peterbilt Atlantic is a heavy trucks dealership with location in Saint-Pascal and Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha!, QC, Charlottetown, PE, Kentville, Sydney, and Dartmouth, NS, Deer Lake, NL, and Hanwell and Moncton, NB. We sell new & used heavy trucks and trailers. Offering parts, service, and financing, near Quebec, Prince Edward Island, Nova Scotia, Newfoundland and Labrador, and New Brunswick."

**Imagery described:** The homepage visual language across the site prominently features Peterbilt trucks (especially the aerodynamic 579 sleeper cab and the classic-styled 389/589 long-nose models) in professional photography, often parked in front of large modern service facilities bearing the Peterbilt Red Oval logo. Facility imagery shows wide service-bay drive-throughs and comfortable lounge areas. The Peterbilt corporate colour palette (red, black, chrome) dominates throughout.

## 3. Locations

The site describes **nine** locations across five provinces. The meta description references Saint-Pascal and Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha! in QC, Charlottetown in PE, Kentville, Sydney and Dartmouth in NS, Deer Lake in NL, and Hanwell and Moncton in NB. Individual location sub-pages (e.g., `/hanwell`, `/moncton`) do not expose unique content — they resolve to the homepage shell — but location-specific data is available from blog posts, the staff page, and third-party sources.

### Hanwell, New Brunswick (Headquarters)
- **Address:** 125 Greenview Drive, Hanwell, NB E3C (TransCanada Exit 281)
- **Phone:** (506) 452-7946 / Toll-free: 1-800-922-7383
- **Hours:** Mon–Fri 7:00 AM–10:00 PM; Sat–Sun Closed ([Trucker Guide](https://truckerguideapp.com/peterbilt/peterbilt-atlantic))
- The **original Fredericton location** was at 45 Gillis Road; the new Hanwell facility opened **June 12, 2017** ([blog post](https://www.peterbiltatlantic.com/blog/new-peterbilt-atlantic-fredericton-location-open-june-12th--21568)). The building was described as featuring an innovative drive-through "Express Check-In" bay, the first in the region.

### Moncton, New Brunswick
- Located in the Caledonia Industrial Park area
- Phone: (506) 854-7383
- One of the earliest expansion locations; the formal "Peterbilt Atlantic" Moncton opening was celebrated in 2014 with a ribbon-cutting, featuring a display of the then-new Peterbilt 587 ([Truck News, August 2014](https://www.trucknews.com/transportation/east-coast-dealer-celebrates-new-digs-with-pete-n-greet/1003120730/))
- The facility includes a 36,000 sq ft indoor showroom, 12 service bays, and a driver's lounge (per the 2014 Truck News report)

### Charlottetown, Prince Edward Island
- Peterbilt's sole PEI presence; the Hawkins family holds Peterbilt rights to PEI

### Dartmouth, Nova Scotia
- **Address:** 65 Gloria McCluskey Avenue, Dartmouth, NS
- The new purpose-built Dartmouth location announced August 2024 ([blog post](https://www.peterbiltatlantic.com/blog/new-dartmouth-location--80354)) and opened **November 4, 2024** ([blog category listing](https://www.peterbiltatlantic.com/blog/category/trucks--1228))
- Features new service bays, first-class drivers' lounge, showroom stocked with parts, chrome, and branded clothing, and the "iconic Peterbilt Red Oval overlooking the Burnside Industrial Park"
- Branch Manager: James Teed; contact: 902-468-7768 Ext. 5200

### Kentville, Nova Scotia
- Acquired approximately 2011 ("three years ago" as of the 2014 Truck News article)
- Won a **Platinum Oval Award** (Peterbilt Motors' dealer recognition program) — noted in the News blog category listing

### Sydney, Nova Scotia
- **Address:** 524 Portsway Avenue, Sydport Industrial Park
- **Phone:** (902) 564-0231
- Hours: Mon–Fri 8am–5pm with 24-hr emergency service ([blog post, Nov. 2017](https://www.peterbiltatlantic.com/blog/peterbilt-atlantic-sydney--21619))
- "Only minutes from the Marine Atlantic Terminal"

### Deer Lake, Newfoundland & Labrador

## 4. Service

**URL attempted:** `/service`, `/our-service`, `/truck-service` — all resolve to homepage shell without unique text content.

**From other pages:** Peterbilt Atlantic presents itself as a multi-make service centre accepting "all models." The Hanwell facility (headquarters) features an "Express Check-In" drive-through. Each location staffs certified technicians for PACCAR MX engine and Cummins engine repair. The Parts & Tech Tips page hosts eight video tutorials covering: MX13 Maintenance Intervals, Adjusting Air Ride Suspensions, Dashboard Lights, DPF SCR Systems, MX Engine Maintenance, Inspecting/Replacing Flex Exhaust Pipe, SmartLINQ, and Winter Maintenance.
