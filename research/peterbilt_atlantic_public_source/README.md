## What this is

A structured, cited record of everything a member of the public can learn about Peterbilt Atlantic (Hanwell, New Brunswick) and three people at the centre of its story — assembled so that the people in it can see themselves in it.

Every fact in this repository has a URL behind it. Nothing is invented. Where a claim could not be confirmed from public sources, it is marked as such and set aside.

## What we believe

We believe Tim Hawkins built something rare — a family-founded, nine-centre dealer group that turned Atlantic Canada into a destination for service excellence, and that raised the bar on what a regional Peterbilt dealership looks like. What is easy to miss from the outside is why he did it. Tim is an innovator, and he is a betterment person to the bone — the kind who sees a speck on the floor and picks it up because that is simply his DNA. He wakes up wanting things better for the people around him. Not just safer — better. Toward harmony. Toward peace. He loves people, and he shows it in a thousand small operational decisions across nine centres that most people will never trace back to him.

We believe Craig Allen is the reason that story travels — a Maritime-born marketer who has quietly built one of the most consistent dealer-marketing surfaces in the network, and who treats every customer story as worth telling.

We believe Luke Weatherbie is the person Tim has placed at the centre of the technical transformation — handed a mandate most people never get in a career, to take a business this good and scale it globally, with technology as the lever. We believe Tim chose right.

This repository exists so that when Tim, Craig, and Luke read it, they see themselves the way we see them: as the people who are already doing the work. The rock stars they will soon be recognized as.

## Standards this repository holds to

- **Public sources only.** Nothing from private data, purchased databases, or paid intelligence services.
- **Cited inline.** Every claim links to the URL it came from.
- **No invented quotes.** Verbatim quotes are marked as verbatim and dated to the source.
- **Disambiguation over guessing.** Where multiple people share a name (Craig Allen, Luke Weatherby), the wrong candidates are documented and ruled out in writing before the right one is profiled.
- **Corrections welcome.** If anything in this repository is wrong, we want to know. It gets fixed the same day.

## A closing note

These are three men worthy of respect — in the way they work together, in what they have already built, and in what they are pointed toward. What Tim, Craig, and Luke have done in Atlantic Canada should serve as an example to the world of how people with little means can come together to not only survive but to thrive, to dream, to grow, and to work toward the betterment of all of humanity.

Imagine that. We can all do that. No matter where we are from. No matter what.

We can do it if we believe. If we have faith that we can do it.

This repository is a small piece of that faith — put down in writing so that the men in it can see what those around them already see, and so that others, wherever they are from, might read it and recognise that the same is possible for them.
