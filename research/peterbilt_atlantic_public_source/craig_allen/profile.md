## Overview

Craig Allen is the Director of Sales & Marketing at Peterbilt Atlantic, a role he has held since October 2016. He is described on the [official staff page](https://www.peterbiltatlantic.com/view-our--xstaff) as someone who "leads the Peterbilt Atlantic sales & marketing department" with "extensive expertise in digital communications" and as "a true east coaster and a long time fixture on the Maritime business scene." The French-language staff page describes him identically: "Craig Allen dirige le service marketing de Peterbilt Atlantic avec une vaste expertise en communications numériques... un incontournable de longue date sur la scène du marketing des Maritimes" ([fr.peterbiltatlantic.com](https://fr.peterbiltatlantic.com/view-our--xstaff)).

Prior to joining Peterbilt Atlantic, Craig ran his own digital marketing agency for seven and a half years and had a previous marketing career at a regional web technology firm and a strategic marketing agency.

## Career History

### Director of Sales & Marketing — Peterbilt Atlantic (October 2016 – present)

Craig joined Peterbilt Atlantic in October 2016 ([LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jcraigallen)). His role encompasses sales management, digital marketing strategy, public communications, and media contact for the dealership network. His name and contact information appear on numerous blog posts, press releases, and marketing campaigns (see marketing_footprint.md). His LinkedIn self-description reads: "Marketing specialist with particular expertise in digital strategy."

### Owner — Craig Allen Agency (April 2009 – September 2016; ~7.5 years)

Prior to joining Peterbilt Atlantic, Craig operated his own New Brunswick-focused digital marketing agency. His LinkedIn describes its scope: "The Craig Allen Agency offers New Brunswick companies both small and large strategy & service in web marketing, social media and online customer engagement. A particular specialty is the development & management of customized social networks for brands and organizations." The agency ran for approximately 7.5 years before Craig transitioned to the Peterbilt Atlantic role.

### Director of Marketing — Evolving Solutions (December 2005 – August 2008; ~2.75 years)

Craig served as Director of Marketing at Evolving Solutions, a regional web technology firm, where he "led the marketing, branding and sales strategy for the firm's regional web business products and services including the launching of the firm's flagship Big Santo product." He describes spending "considerable time working in the emerging 'Social Media and Web 2.0' space learning how to maximize the power of applications such as Facebook, Twitter and Blogs" ([LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jcraigallen)).

### Account Manager — Vaka Strategic Marketing (August 2002 – November 2005; ~3.25 years)

Craig led "account teams in developing and executing marketing strategies for a diverse range of Maritime-based firms seeking to expand their product and service offerings in regional, national and international markets." The role included directing client services, developing strategic marketing plans, and building strategic alliances ([LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jcraigallen)).
