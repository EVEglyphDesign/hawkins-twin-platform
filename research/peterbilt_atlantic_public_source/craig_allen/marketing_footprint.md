## "Built in Canada — Driven by You" Campaign

Craig Allen is the named contact for Peterbilt Atlantic's campaign announcing that Class 8 Peterbilt trucks are now built at PACCAR's Sainte-Thérèse, Quebec facility — making Peterbilt–PACCAR the only OEM building both heavy- and medium-duty trucks in Canada. The post, published in both English and French, closes:

> "Interested in learning more? Please contact Craig Allen, Director of Sales & Marketing at 506.451.2001 / callen@peterbiltatlantic.com or any Peterbilt Atlantic sales rep at 1-800-922-7383"

Sources: [peterbiltatlantic.com](https://www.peterbiltatlantic.com/blog/built-in-canada---driven-by-you--101690); [fr.peterbiltatlantic.com](https://fr.peterbiltatlantic.com/blog/built-in-canada---driven-by-you--101690) (October 26, 2025)

## UNB Student Partnership (2021)

Craig co-led the partnership between Peterbilt Atlantic and UNB's Faculty of Management Strategic Management course in 2021. Fourth-year students conducted primary and secondary research into EV truck market penetration in Eastern Canada. Craig is named as the company-side supervisor in the UNB blog.

His publicly quoted statement from that project:

> "It was very evident that the students took their projects seriously and we were very impressed with the quality of their work and recommendations. It was great to see the students embrace the challenge of researching a new sector and fledging product with such enthusiasm."

Source: [UNB blog, July 30, 2021](https://blogs.unb.ca/ideas-with-impact/2021/09/peterbilt.php)

## Note on LinkedIn "Meet the Expert" YouTube Reference

The brief references a "Meet the Expert" YouTube on getting noticed on LinkedIn. A search found a YouTube video titled "Meet the Expert: Craig Allan — How to Get Noticed on LinkedIn" ([youtube.com/watch?v=LJ9w7e32bqk](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LJ9w7e32bqk), January 14, 2024). The transcript of that video identifies the presenter as "Craig Allen... a business and executive coach at The Power Within." This is **not** the Peterbilt Atlantic Craig Allen — this Craig Allan is UK-based, coaches executives, and operates via "The Power Within." The Peterbilt Atlantic Craig Allen's LinkedIn handle is *jcraigallen* (Fredericton, NB). No "Meet the Expert" video featuring the Peterbilt Atlantic Craig Allen on LinkedIn strategy was located in public search results at time of research.
