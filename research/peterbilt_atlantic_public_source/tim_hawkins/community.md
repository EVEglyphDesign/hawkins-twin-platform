## Peterbilt Proud Program

Peterbilt Atlantic operates a formal community initiative called **Peterbilt Proud**, described on the company's community page as the umbrella for charitable giving, event sponsorships, and education partnerships. The program "focuses directly on giving back to communities we live in" (Tim Hawkins, [LDANB press release, Feb. 2019](https://www.ldanb-taanb.ca/peterbilt-atlantic-sponsors-learning-disabilities-association-of-new-brunswick-program-expansion/)).

## Learning Disabilities Association of New Brunswick (LDANB)

In February 2019, Peterbilt Atlantic — under Tim Hawkins' leadership — became the **signature sponsor** of the LDANB's expansion of the Barton Reading and Spelling System to the Moncton area. The program is based on the Orton-Gillingham method and was designed to serve 25–30 students with dyslexia and other learning disabilities. The press release confirms that 100% of Peterbilt Atlantic's contribution went directly to the program expansion ([LDANB-TAANB](https://www.ldanb-taanb.ca/peterbilt-atlantic-sponsors-learning-disabilities-association-of-new-brunswick-program-expansion/)).

Tim's personal statement: *"At Peterbilt Atlantic we believe that education is a cornerstone for success."*

## UNB Partnership

Tim Hawkins, as a UNB alumnus (BBA '91), co-led a work-integrated learning partnership with UNB's Faculty of Management in 2021. UNB Strategic Management students analyzed opportunities for Peterbilt Atlantic to increase EV truck market penetration in Eastern Canada. Craig Allen, Director of Sales & Marketing, supervised the students' day-to-day engagement ([UNB blog, July 30, 2021](https://blogs.unb.ca/ideas-with-impact/2021/09/peterbilt.php)).

## Alma Mater Engagement — University of Western Ontario / Delta Upsilon

On September 25, 2021, Tim returned to his undergraduate alma mater (University of Western Ontario, BA '88) to deliver a shipment of Moosehead beer to the Delta Upsilon fraternity house in London, Ontario, using a classic Peterbilt Model 359 operated by OBYR Transport. The event was tied to a ribbon-cutting ceremony for the refurbishment of the fraternity house. Tim and Peterbilt Atlantic also thanked Moosehead Breweries for partnering on the gesture ([Peterbilt Atlantic blog, September 27, 2021](https://www.peterbiltatlantic.com/blog/special-delivery-at-western--40420)).

## UNB Reds Hockey

Peterbilt Atlantic is a named sponsor of UNB hockey — a program that has won seven national championships. The sponsorship covers travel, training, and ice time. The company also sponsors the Peterbilt Atlantic zamboni at the UNB rink and supports the annual Peterbilt Pete Kelly Cup tournament in Fredericton at Christmas ([peterbiltatlantic.com/community](https://www.peterbiltatlantic.com/check-out-our--community)). Tim's personal connection to UNB as an alumnus adds a dimension of alumni loyalty to this corporate commitment.
