## On Peterbilt Atlantic's Mission (2014)

> "Atlantic Canada is fast becoming a hub for truck service excellence, and Peterbilt Atlantic is determined to lead the charge."

**Source:** [Truck News — "East-coast dealer celebrates new digs with Pete 'n' greet"](https://www.trucknews.com/transportation/east-coast-dealer-celebrates-new-digs-with-pete-n-greet/1003120730/), August 2014.
**Context:** Spoken to local media at the ribbon-cutting ceremony for the new Peterbilt Atlantic Moncton facility.

## On LDANB Sponsorship (2019)

> "On behalf of the entire staff of Peterbilt Atlantic, it gives us great pride to be able to provide access to this outstanding program for students in the Moncton area. At Peterbilt Atlantic we believe that education is a cornerstone for success and we are honoured to assist the excellent work of the LDANB in this province. By removing barriers to education in society, we know that the entire community benefits immensely. This support as part of our Peterbilt Proud program focuses directly on giving back to communities we live in. We look forward to assisting the LDANB grow their programming around New Brunswick in years to come."

**Source:** [LDANB-TAANB press release — "Peterbilt Atlantic Sponsors Learning Disabilities Association of New Brunswick Program Expansion"](https://www.ldanb-taanb.ca/peterbilt-atlantic-sponsors-learning-disabilities-association-of-new-brunswick-program-expansion/), February 6, 2019.
**Context:** Announcement of Peterbilt Atlantic as signature sponsor for the expansion of the Barton Tutoring Program to the Moncton region.
