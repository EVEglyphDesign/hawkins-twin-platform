Welcome, Luke. This is your landing page for Module 1 of your training as the designated enterprise consultant for the Hawkins twin.

## The tone we set here

We are ultra-compliance. That means:

- We do not ask anyone — you, us, Craig, Tim, or anyone at Hawkins — to touch material they are not entitled to touch.
- We treat every vendor we pay (CDK Global, PACCAR, BRP, and others) with the same respect for their agreements that we expect them to show for ours.
- When in doubt, we stop and ask. Silence-then-ask beats guess-and-apologize every time.

You will **never** be asked in this program to violate any agreement you have signed. If anything in this repository ever appears to conflict with an agreement you are subject to, stop and raise it with Dany Theriault and Tim Hawkins. That path is always open and always correct.

## What Module 1 delivers

By the end of Module 1 you will have produced a compliant, EVE-Glyph-shaped mirror of the two inventory systems most relevant to Peterbilt Atlantic / Hawkins:

1. **BRP / CDK Global side** — the DMS you are currently being certified on
2. **Peterbilt / PACCAR side** — the parts and dealer systems Craig Allen described in his systems register

The mirror pattern is the same one we use for the SAP twin: public API and documentation on one side, your own sanctioned exports and personal notes on the other, and a Postgres schema that lets both sides join cleanly downstream.
