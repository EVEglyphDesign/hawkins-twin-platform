## Person Summaries

### Tim Hawkins — `tim_hawkins/`

Tim Hawkins is the founder and CEO of Peterbilt Atlantic, the largest independent heavy-duty truck dealership in Atlantic Canada, and the CEO of Extreme Torque Motorsports (Torque Motorsports), a sister powersports company — both headquartered in Hanwell / Fredericton, New Brunswick. He holds a BA in Economics from the University of Western Ontario (1988) and a BBA from the University of New Brunswick (1991), and was a member of the Delta Upsilon fraternity at Western. He and his father launched the first Fredericton Peterbilt dealership in 1994, building on a family trucking legacy of over 50 years; by the time Peterbilt Atlantic was formally launched as an umbrella brand in August 2014, the group had grown to six locations, and today operates nine centres across New Brunswick, Nova Scotia, PEI, Newfoundland and Labrador, and Quebec. Under Tim's leadership, Peterbilt Atlantic has invested in community through the Peterbilt Proud programme — including a signature sponsorship of the LDANB Barton Tutoring Program (2019), UNB Reds Hockey support, and a 2021 work-integrated learning partnership with UNB students researching EV truck market entry. His publicly recorded quote in the 2014 Truck News coverage captures his orientation: *"Atlantic Canada is fast becoming a hub for truck service excellence, and Peterbilt Atlantic is determined to lead the charge."*

### Craig Allen — `craig_allen/`

Craig Allen is the Director of Sales & Marketing at Peterbilt Atlantic, a role he has held since October 2016, and is based in Fredericton, New Brunswick. He holds a BA in Anthropology and English from UNB (graduated ~1998) and a credential in International Business from NBCC, and lists Spanish as a language. Before joining Peterbilt Atlantic he operated the Craig Allen Agency, a New Brunswick-focused digital marketing consultancy (2009–2016), and earlier served as Director of Marketing at Evolving Solutions and as an account manager at Vaka Strategic Marketing — giving him roughly two decades of Maritime marketing experience. At Peterbilt Atlantic he is the primary author and contact for the company's blog content, campaign launches (including the EV Calculator campaign, the "Built in Canada — Driven by You" campaign, and the Peterbilt Atlantic Academy internship programme), and press releases; he co-led the 2021 UNB student partnership with Tim Hawkins and introduced the Peterbilt Atlantic Speaker Series on YouTube. His staff biography, in both English and French, describes him as carrying "extensive expertise in digital communications" and as a "long time fixture on the Maritime business scene." Outside work, the staff page notes he enjoys salmon fishing and alpine skiing.
